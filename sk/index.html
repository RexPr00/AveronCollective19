What could go wrong and how we reduce risk

Every investment has risk. Metal prices can fall, policy shifts can change demand, and short-term volatility can affect returns. We keep risk control practical and visible.

Price shock / High impact

Action: staged exits and hedge use. Baseline: no hedge plan. Investor gain: reduced downside pressure.

Rate surprise / Medium impact

Action: rebalance into defensive sleeve. Baseline: static weights. Investor gain: better flexibility.

Supply disruption / Medium impact

Action: rotate into resilient producers. Baseline: fixed holdings. Investor gain: faster recovery path.

Sentiment reversal / Lower impact

Action: cut leverage and hold cash. Baseline: full exposure. Investor gain: less stress during pullbacks.
